Welcome to Tacpas Global Learning

Pharmacovigilance (PV) is a dynamic and exciting career path for life science and pharmacy graduates with clearly defined milestones. The growth is comparatively faster eventually leading to various Leadership roles within few years. So as a Pharmacy/ Healthcare student, are you “Market Ready” to take this career leap? At Tacpas, We are focused on one goal: to develop you as a well-trained and effective workforce for this fast-growing PV industry. We aim to accomplish this by providing trainings and educating students on fundamentals of product safety and regulatory compliance and conducting a comprehensive, yet practical assessment of the all the verticals of PV required to create a “Tailor-made” PV Safety Scientist in you. We will conduct workshops for individuals on safety, clinical, regulatory related activities, processes and documentation.

Carrer Oppertunities

  • What will you achieve?

    There are a lot of students who now go in for pharmacy graduation due to the sheer width of opportunities that lie before them. There used to be a time where pharmacy students used to become medical representatives or open up medical stores. The scenario has rapidly changed as there are a wide host of options in terms of Research and development of pharma products or look at a career in Quality Assurance, Regulatory Affairs or Pharmacovigilance and even Quality Control in drugs companies.

    Other than expanding your global safety knowledge, you will enhance your capabilities and compliance in both the regulations and PV Industry’s expectations. With our cumulative experience of more than 20 years in this field, we bring you multiple job opportunities in both Pharmaceutical Companies and Contract Research Organisations (CROs). We also aim to provide a contract services to Pharmaceutical companies and other Contract Research Organisations (CROs) on their safety, regulatory, clinical and medical affairs related activities, processes and documentation. We can help ensure you build and maintain a quality pharmacovigilance department ready for any pharmacovigilance inspections

  • Is training platform accessible from any location?

    Fresher Level job in Pharmacovigilance is DSA (Drug Safety Associate). A Drug safety Associate is mainly involved in case creation, checking for MSI (Minimum safety information - a patient, a reporter, a suspect drug and a adverse event), reconciliation and follow up process, data entry of all information available in the document and medical coding

    3 Years of experience builds more skill sets as (Medical coding, narrative and scientific writing, good understanding of medical terms and basic understanding of regulatory affairs, ICH-GCP and compliance etc) which can bag him a position as DSS (Drug Safety Scientist).

    With a Certification in Pharmacovigilance and an overall of 5+ Years experience, candidates can become a Team lead or Team manager based on the company.

  • Eligibility Criteria?

    Graduates in any stream are eligible to apply for PHARMACOVIGILANCE Training Course.
